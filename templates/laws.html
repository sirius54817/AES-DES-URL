Information Technology Act, 2000

The IT Act provides legal recognition to electronic documents and digital signatures. It addresses:

  • Cybercrime and fraud prevention
  • Digital signatures and electronic records
  • E-governance and e-commerce regulations

Penalties and Punishments

Unauthorized Access

Up to ₹1 crore fine and imprisonment

Data Theft

Up to 3 years imprisonment or fine up to ₹5 lakhs

Identity Theft

Up to 3 years imprisonment and fine up to ₹1 lakh
